What is the Knights Tour?

A Knight's Tour is a path across a chessboard made of only legal moves a knight can take in the game of chess such that it visits every tile on the board exactly once. It is an example of a Hamiltonian Cycle. Read more about them here.

How does a Knight Move?

<

A knight has exactly 8 legal moves, shaped like an 'L', from its starting position. In Chess, a Knight may pass-over, or "jump" other pieces, but that isn't important for now. Read more about them here.
